Book Worm

  • High Concept: Worm Must Eat Books!!!
  • Theme: RPG.
  • Mandated Variety: Book position is random.(Randomness)
  • Player Experience Goals: Player will feel hungry. lol
  • Gameplay: Player must walk around and eat books. When the worm stands on top of a book, pressing x will make the worm start eating the book. Reach 2000 points to win.
  • Prototype Goal: Manage multiple eatable items and properly remove selected one.
  • Features: PUNS!!!
  • Setting/Premise: Worm is at a green field of books!
  • Story: Worm eats old books.
  • Target Audience: CS325 People and kids of all ages!
  • Play Time: Player has to reach 2000 Points.
  • Strategies: Speed and a little hacking :)
