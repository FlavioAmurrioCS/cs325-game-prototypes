Asteroid Shooting Cowboy

  • High Concept: Asteroid spins around while shooting mini cowboys into space.
  • Theme: Asteroid lost in the vod of space.
  • Mandated Variety: Asteroid rotation follows mouse.
  • Player Experience Goals: Enjoy the music and laugh a little.
  • Gameplay: Use mouse to move the asteroid around. Left-Click mouse to shoot. Press up-down arrows to control music volume.
  • Prototype Goal: The effectiveness of using sound.
  • Features: Cowboy in space.
  • Setting/Premise: Asteroid get colonized my cowboy clones. Cowboys play music. Asteroid gets pumped with the music and it start ejecting the cowboys into the void of space.
  • Story: Look above^^^.
  • Target Audience: CS325 Students.
  • Play Time: Fun never stops.
  • Strategies: Shoot as fast as possible.
