Make Game Design Great Again

  • High Concept: Get most amount of money.
  • Theme: SideScroller.
  • Mandated Variety: SideScroller, Predetermined game layout.
  • Player Experience Goals: Laugh at our president.
  • Gameplay: Player is constantly running. Player can jump and duck.
  • Prototype Goal: Trying out Tile Map Editor.
  • Features: Funny Graphics. :)
  • Setting/Premise: Trump is sent to an open field where he must gather money in order to become president of the United Nations. He must do so while avoiding taxes as taxes are his weaksness.
  • Story: Future iterations may containt press people coming to him and he will have a catch phrase.
  • Target Audience: American people.
  • Play Time: 30 seconds.
  • Strategies: Player must time jumps as to avoid falling into pits and taxes.
